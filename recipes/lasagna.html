World's Best Lasagna


This famous and delicious dish is surprisingly easy to prepare.
Originating from Naples, Italy, this dish has been a staple of
Italian cuisine since the XIV century, being an important nutritional source
during the Black Plague. Ironically, the dish was invented when the Plague
started.

The dish is comprised of layers of minced meat, seasoned tomato sauce,
and cheese topped off with lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and/or lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
