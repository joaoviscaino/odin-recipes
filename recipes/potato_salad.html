Creamy Potato Salad


Potato salad is an European dish. It created in the 16th century, believed
to have originated in Germany.

This dish is simple and cheap, especially for how delicious it tastes.
It's a must-have in any family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized potatoes
  • 1/2 cup white of sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • salt
  • 5 ounces of evaporated milk (can)
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 6 large hard-cooked eggs, diced
  • 1 small onion, finelly chopped
  • 1 cup of celery

Steps

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
    Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
    Drain and set aside to cool.
  2. While the potatoes are cooking, whisk together sugar, 2 beaten eggs,
    and cornstarch in a saucepan; season with salt.
  3. Stir in milk, vinegar, and mustard. Cook and stir over medium heat
    until thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in butter.
    Refrigerate until cool, then stir in mayonnaise.
  4. Peel and dice potatoes. Transfer to a large bowl; toss with hard-cooked
    eggs, onion, celery, and 1 teaspoon salt.
  5. Stir dressing gently into the bowl of potato salad until evenly coated.
  6. Chill before serving, 3 hours to overnight.
